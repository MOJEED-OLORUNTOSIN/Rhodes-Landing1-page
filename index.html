News Update

Population Growth

Africa's population is expected to reach 2.5 billion by 2050, with Nigeria projected to become the third most populous country globally by then, according to the United Nations.

Economic growth

According to the African Development Bank (AfDB), Africa's economy is projected to grow by 3.4% in 2024, up from 3.1% in 2023. This growth is driven by recovery in commodity prices, infrastructure investments, and reforms in several economies.

Inflation Rate

Nigeria's inflation rate was 15.7% in June 2024, down from 16.5% in May 2024, reflecting improved stability in food prices and efforts by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) to manage monetary policy.
